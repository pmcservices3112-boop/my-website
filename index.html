Our Services

Society Formation & Compliances

  • Society Registration – Efficient assistance for new housing societies.
  • Statutory Registers – Maintenance of:
  • 1. Share certificate
  • 2. Share register
  • 3. I-form register
  • 4. J-form register
  • 5. Nomination forms and register
  • 6. Investment register

Financial Management & Accounting

  • Maintenance Billing & Receipts – Monthly/Quarterly bills & receipts via Email and WhatsApp.
  • Accounts Finalization – Annual accounts prepared to standards:
    • 1. Income and Expenditure
    • 2. Balance sheet
    • 3. Member dues annexure
    • 4. Investment annexure
    • 5. Fixed asset annexure
  • Audit Services – Statutory audits for transparency and compliance.

Advisory & Support Services

  • Regulatory Guidance – Expert advice on housing society laws.
  • TDS and GST compliance – TDS filing, GST returns and regulatory compliance
  • Housing society ITR filing – Simplified and accurate ITR filing for housing societies and timely compliance.
  • Customized Solutions – Tailored services for every society.
  • Ongoing Support – Continuous assistance for smooth operations.
